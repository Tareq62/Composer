Tareq Abuissa is a composer, lyricist, and multi-instrumentalist. While studying Music and Mathematics at Columbia University, he co-composed the 118th Annual Varsity Show, The Corporate Core. His work for chamber ensemble, animation, and dance has been presented at Columbia University’s Miller Theatre, University of Southern California, and Barnard College, respectively. His 10-track studio album debut, Echoing Green, will be released in 2017. He is the composer-lyricist and music director for South of Market: The Musical, an annual satire on San Francisco’s tech industry. Abuissa is a member of the Society of Composers & Lyricists.
