Tareq Abuissa is a composer, lyricist, and multi-instrumentalist. While studying Music and Mathematics at Columbia University, he co-composed the score and lyrics for the 118th Annual Varsity Show, The Corporate Core. His work for chamber ensemble, film, and dance has been presented at Columbia University’s Miller Theatre, University of Southern California, and Barnard College, respectively. His 10-track studio album debut, Echoing Green, will be released in 2017. He composes and music directs South of Market: The Musical, an annual satire of San Francisco’s tech industry. Abuissa is a member of the Society of Composers & Lyricists.
